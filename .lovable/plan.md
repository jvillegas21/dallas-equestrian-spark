

# Dallas Equestrian Center — Modern Website

A polished, multi-page website for Dallas Equestrian Center that reflects the elegance and sophistication the equestrian community expects. The site will feature rich imagery, refined typography, and a warm yet professional aesthetic.

---

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Home Page
- Hero section with a stunning full-width photo/video banner and a compelling tagline
- Overview of key services (Riding Lessons, Boarding, Training, Events) with icons and brief descriptions
- Featured testimonials or highlights
- Call-to-action buttons (Book a Lesson, Contact Us)
- Quick-glance stats (years in business, acres, horses trained, etc.)

### 2. About Us
- The story of Dallas Equestrian Center — history, mission, and values
- Meet the team/trainers section with photos and bios
- Facility highlights and what sets you apart

### 3. Riding Lessons
- Detailed breakdown of lesson types (beginner, intermediate, advanced, youth, adult)
- What to expect during a lesson
- Call-to-action to book a lesson

### 4. Horse Boarding
- Boarding packages and amenities (stall types, turnout, feeding programs)
- Facility features (arenas, trails, pastures)
- Call-to-action to inquire about boarding

### 5. Training Programs
- Overview of training philosophy and methods
- Types of training offered
- Trainer credentials and experience

### 6. Events & Shows
- Upcoming events, clinics, and competitions
- Past event highlights with photos
- How to participate or attend

### 7. Gallery
- Photo and video gallery showcasing the facility, horses, lessons, and events
- Organized into categories (Facility, Lessons, Events, Horses)

### 8. Pricing & Packages
- Clear pricing tables for lessons, boarding, and training
- Membership or package options if applicable
- FAQ section addressing common pricing questions

### 9. Contact & Booking
- Booking request form (name, email, phone, service of interest, preferred date/time, message)
- Contact information (address, phone, email)
- Embedded map showing the Dallas location
- Business hours

---

## Design Direction
- **Style**: Modern, elegant, and editorial — think clean lines, generous whitespace, and sophisticated typography
- **Imagery**: Large, high-quality equestrian photography throughout
- **Color palette**: Will incorporate your existing brand colors and logo
- **Typography**: Refined serif headings paired with clean sans-serif body text
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly with smooth navigation

## Navigation
- Sticky top navigation bar with your logo and page links
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Consistent footer with contact info, quick links, and social media icons

## No Backend Required
- Static informational site with a booking request form (submissions handled via a simple email notification or form service)
- All content is managed directly in the codebase — no CMS needed
